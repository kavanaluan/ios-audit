Built For Real Writing Workflows

WritelyAI is not designed to make AI write blindly. It helps AI understand the current project, chapter target, selected text, and your editing intent.

Chapter-Level Context

Generate results around the current chapter, selected passage, and pinned materials, so revisions stay closer to the writing target.

Voice Writing

When you start voice input, WritelyAI can turn speech into writing instructions or draft text for quick idea capture.

Review And Feedback

Preview AI output before applying it, accept or reject changes, and send feedback when something needs support.

How It Works

Move from notes to manuscript while keeping each step reviewable, editable, and recoverable.

Collect Notes And Outline

Add worldbuilding, characters, structure, and reference material to the project.

Shape Chapter Targets

Use the chapter list as the manuscript structure and move through it in writing order.

Ask AI For Focused Help

Select a passage or chapter before generating, rewriting, expanding, or summarizing.

Review Before Applying

Apply results only after review, and use timeline snapshots when you need to recover prior work.
